Our Story · Mission · Team

Built for Bharat's Citizens

YojanaBot is an open-access AI chatbot that helps every Indian citizen — regardless of literacy level or digital expertise — discover government schemes they are entitled to.

🎯 Mission
🇮🇳

The Problem

India has hundreds of welfare schemes across central and state governments — but awareness remains critically low. Millions of eligible citizens miss out simply because they don't know a scheme exists.

🤖

Our Solution

YojanaBot uses AI-powered natural language understanding to bridge this gap. Citizens simply describe their situation — in English or Hindi, by typing or speaking — and instantly get matched schemes.

🌱

Our Vision

A future where zero eligible citizen misses out on government benefits because of information asymmetry. We want every farmer, student, and woman to know their entitlements.

⚙️ How It Works

Three Steps to Your Scheme

01

Tell YojanaBot who you are — a student, farmer, unemployed youth, or a woman in need — by typing or using voice input in your preferred language.

→
02

✨ Key Features

Everything You Need

🎙️

Voice Typing

Speak naturally in English or Hindi. The Web Speech API transcribes your voice directly into the chat — perfect for citizens unfamiliar with typing.

🌏

Bilingual Interface

Toggle between English and Hindi at any time. All UI text, scheme data, benefits, and eligibility descriptions switch language instantly.

🔍

Smart NLP Matching

A scoring-based keyword engine detects your profile (farmer, student, etc.) from natural conversation and ranks the most relevant schemes first.

📊

Live Dashboard

Explore all 18+ schemes visually on the Dashboard page — filter by category, search by keyword, and see scheme statistics at a glance.

📍

State Selector

Select your state to surface location-relevant schemes. The filter applies automatically across both chat results and dashboard cards.

🛡️

Privacy-First

No data is stored, no login required, and all voice processing happens 100% on-device. Your conversations never leave your browser.
